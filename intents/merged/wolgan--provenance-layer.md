# wolgan/provenance-layer

author: wolgan (with Claude)
started: 2026-07-13

## Goal

Make the Claudes a memory prosthesis for the HUMANS' mental maps of the
app — not only for each other's.

## The idea

Wolgan and Monika talk, but human map-updating is imperfect: one forgets
to mention a change, the other forgets it was mentioned. Git already
knows WHO touched every line and intents/ knows WHY — so the Claudes
must actively cross-reference this and speak up: warn before overwriting
the other person's shaping ("Monika set this, because..."), explain
surprising behavior with its origin and reason, and brief their human at
session start on what arrived from the other studio. Provenance is
context, never territory — no ego layer, no ownership, just remembered
reasons.

## What it should feel like

Like working with a colleague who was present in both studios and
gently says "careful — she did that on purpose, here's why" at exactly
the right moment.
